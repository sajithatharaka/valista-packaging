Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Valista Packaging ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to remember choices you've made.

How we use cookies

This website currently uses no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking scripts. The only client-side storage we use is a single, strictly necessary item that remembers whether you've dismissed the cookie notice on this site, stored using your browser's local storage rather than a traditional cookie. It contains no personal information and is not shared with any third party.

If we add analytics or marketing tools in the future, this policy will be updated in advance to reflect exactly what is added and why.

Categories of cookies

  • Strictly necessary — the cookie-notice dismissal flag described above. Essential for the site to remember your preference; cannot be disabled without also disabling the notice.
  • Analytics — none currently in use on this website.
  • Marketing — none currently in use on this website.

How to control cookies

Most web browsers let you control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Since this site does not use tracking or analytics cookies, disabling cookies in your browser will not affect your ability to browse this website — it will only cause the cookie notice to reappear on future visits.
