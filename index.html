About Valista

Quality that meets industrial standards. Pricing that doesn’t punish you for it.

Valista Packaging manufactures premium corrugated cartons with cutting-edge technology and an unwavering commitment to quality — making us one of Sri Lanka’s leading producers.

We specialize in 3-ply and 5-ply board, printed or plain, plus die-cut boxes, partitions, and separators built to the tolerances industrial buyers demand. Innovation and sustainability are built into every design, not bolted on.

And since packaging is a cost line, not a luxury, we offer the most competitive prices in Sri Lanka’s packaging industry — without cutting specification.

What We Manufacture

Four product lines, one production standard

Every carton — regardless of ply or finish — runs through the same quality checks before it leaves New Kandy Road.

01 — CORE PRODUCT

3-Ply Cartons

Printed & unprinted. Lighter-duty corrugated board for retail packaging, general shipping cartons, and point-of-sale units.

02

5-Ply Cartons

Printed & unprinted. Heavier board for export shipments, industrial loads, and stacked warehouse storage.

03

Die-Cut Boxes

Custom-cut shapes and fold patterns, precision-tooled for products that don’t fit a standard carton.

04 — INTERNAL PROTECTION

Partitions & Separators

Internal dividers that keep bottles, jars, and fragile units from touching each other in transit and storage.
