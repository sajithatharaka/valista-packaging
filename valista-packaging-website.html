About Valista

Quality that meets industrial standards. Pricing that doesn’t punish you for it.

Valista Packaging manufactures premium corrugated cartons using cutting-edge technology, with a constant commitment to quality that’s kept us among Sri Lanka’s leading producers in the category.

Our specialty is 3-ply and 5-ply board — printed or left unprinted — alongside die-cut boxes and partitions and separators built to the tolerances industrial buyers actually check. Innovation and sustainability sit at the center of how we design a carton, not as an afterthought.

And because packaging is a cost line, not a luxury, we hold the most affordable prices available in the Sri Lankan packaging industry — without cutting the specification to get there.

3-Ply Cartons
